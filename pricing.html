Your listing is automatically paused — not deleted. You'll get an email reminder 5 days before it expires. To renew, simply log in to your seller dashboard and pay the same fee again. All your details, photos, and enquiry history are saved, so renewal takes under a minute.

There is no limit. You can post as many properties as you own or manage. Each listing is priced individually — KES 500 per Standard or KES 1,500 per Featured. If you have 5 or more listings, contact us for a bulk discount package.

Plain English summary: We collect the information you give us when you register, post a listing, or contact a seller. We use it to run the platform and never sell your personal data to third parties. You can request to see, correct, or delete your data at any time by emailing hello@nyumbakisumu.co.ke.
Section 1

Overview

NyumbaKisumu ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use NyumbaKisumu, how we use it, and what choices you have.

This policy applies to all users of NyumbaKisumu — including buyers, renters, sellers, and landlords — accessing the platform via any device.

By using NyumbaKisumu you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the platform.

Data controller: NyumbaKisumu operates as the data controller for personal information collected on this platform. For any privacy enquiries contact us at hello@nyumbakisumu.co.ke.
Section 2

Data we collect

We collect personal data in the following ways:

Data type What we collect When
Account info Full name, email address, phone number, password (hashed) When you register as a seller
Listing info Property details, photos, price, area, contact phone number When you post a listing
Enquiry info Buyer name, phone number, and message content When a buyer sends an enquiry
Payment info M-Pesa phone number, transaction reference, amount paid When you pay a listing fee
Usage data Pages visited, search queries, browser type, IP address Automatically when you use the site
Communications Emails or messages sent to our support team When you contact us

We do not collect your national ID number, bank account details, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

Section 3

How we use your data

We use the personal data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To run the platform — creating and managing your account, publishing your listings, and delivering buyer enquiries to you
  • To process payments — verifying M-Pesa transactions and activating listings after payment confirmation
  • To communicate with you — sending listing confirmation emails, payment receipts, and important platform updates
  • To improve the platform — analysing how people use NyumbaKisumu so we can make it better
  • To detect and prevent fraud — identifying suspicious listings or accounts and protecting other users
  • To comply with legal obligations — responding to lawful requests from Kenyan authorities where required
We will never: sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties, send you unsolicited marketing from other businesses, or use your data for any purpose not listed above without your consent.
Section 4

Who we share data with

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Other users (limited): When you post a listing, your contact phone number and listing details are visible to buyers on the platform. Buyers who send you an enquiry share their name, phone number, and message with you.
  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider (Supabase, Inc.) stores your account data and listings securely in the EU region.
  • Safaricom M-Pesa: Payment processing is handled by Safaricom. We share your phone number and payment amount with Safaricom to process the STK Push. We do not store your M-Pesa PIN — this is handled entirely by Safaricom.
  • Netlify: Our hosting provider (Netlify, Inc.) may process request logs including IP addresses as part of normal web hosting operations.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose personal data to Kenyan authorities if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes. We do not use advertising networks that track you across other websites.

Section 5

Data storage & security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in the EU (Ireland). Supabase is SOC 2 compliant and uses encryption at rest and in transit.

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt — we cannot see your password
  • Your M-Pesa PIN is never passed through our systems — it goes directly to Safaricom
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — sellers can only access their own listings and enquiries
  • Admin access to the database is protected by strong authentication
No system is 100% secure. While we work hard to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you suspect your account has been compromised, change your password immediately and contact us at hello@nyumbakisumu.co.ke.
Section 6

Section 7

M-Pesa & payments

When you pay a listing fee via M-Pesa, here is exactly what happens with your data:

  • You provide your M-Pesa phone number on our platform
  • We send your phone number and the payment amount to Safaricom's Daraja API
  • Safaricom sends an STK Push directly to your phone
  • You enter your M-Pesa PIN on your phone — this PIN is never seen or stored by NyumbaKisumu
  • Safaricom confirms the payment to us with a transaction reference number (e.g. QF12AB3CD4)
  • We store the transaction reference, amount, and payment status in our database linked to your listing
We do not store card numbers, bank account details, or your M-Pesa PIN. All payment processing is handled entirely by Safaricom. For M-Pesa payment disputes, contact Safaricom on 234.
Section 8

How long we keep your data

Data type How long we keep it
Account information Until you delete your account, plus 30 days for recovery
Active listings For the duration of the listing (60 or 90 days), then archived
Expired listings 12 months after expiry, then permanently deleted
Buyer enquiries 90 days after the listing expires
Payment records 7 years (required for financial record-keeping under Kenyan law)
Usage / access logs 90 days, then automatically deleted
Support emails 2 years from the date of the last communication

When data is deleted, it is permanently removed from our systems and cannot be recovered. Payment records are the exception — we are required by Kenyan tax and financial regulations to retain these for 7 years.

Section 9

Your rights

You have the following rights over your personal data. To exercise any of them, email us at hello@nyumbakisumu.co.ke and we will respond within 7 business days.

Access Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
Correction Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
Deletion Request deletion of your account and personal data
Objection Object to processing of your data for specific purposes
Portability Request your data in a machine-readable format
Restriction Ask us to limit how we process your data in certain cases

We will process all requests free of charge. In some cases we may be unable to delete certain data (such as payment records required by law) and will explain this clearly.

You can also update or delete your own profile information directly from your seller dashboard at any time.

Section 10

Children's privacy

NyumbaKisumu is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18.

If you believe a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@nyumbakisumu.co.ke and we will delete that information promptly.

Section 11

Third-party links

NyumbaKisumu may contain links to external websites — for example, links to WhatsApp, Google Maps, or other services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those websites.

We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external websites you visit. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

When you click a WhatsApp link on a listing page, you are opening WhatsApp directly and your conversation with the seller is subject to WhatsApp's and Meta's privacy policies.

Section 12

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify registered sellers by email.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of NyumbaKisumu after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Section 13

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@nyumbakisumu.co.ke
  • Subject line: "Privacy Request — [your name]"
  • Response time: Within 7 business days
  • Location: Kisumu, Western Kenya

For M-Pesa payment disputes specifically, contact Safaricom directly on 234 (free from Safaricom lines) as we do not have access to change M-Pesa transaction records.

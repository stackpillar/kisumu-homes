Listing fees are charged to sellers. The current fees are:

Payments are processed via M-Pesa. By making a payment you authorise NyumbaKisumu to charge the stated amount to your M-Pesa account.

Refund policy: Listing fees are non-refundable once your listing has been approved and published. If your listing is rejected by our team, a full refund will be issued within 3 business days. If you experience a payment issue, contact us at hello@nyumbakisumu.co.ke with your M-Pesa transaction reference.

Fees are subject to change. Any fee changes will be announced at least 14 days in advance on the platform. Your existing paid listing duration will not be affected by fee changes.

NyumbaKisumu does not charge commission on sales or rental agreements. The listing fee is the only charge from NyumbaKisumu.

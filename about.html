Our mission

Connect people to homes — without the middleman

Property in Kisumu has always worked the same way: you need a home, you call an agent, the agent calls another agent, and by the time you see the apartment you owe someone a commission before you've even moved in. It's a system that benefits everyone except the people actually looking for a home.

NyumbaKisumu cuts through all of that. Sellers post their properties directly for a flat fee. Buyers browse, shortlist, and contact sellers with one click — no intermediaries, no hidden costs, no wasted afternoons on viewings that were never a good match.

We believe the rental and property market in Western Kenya deserves a modern platform that puts landlords and tenants in the same room, digitally — and then gets out of the way.

How we got here

A Kisumu problem that needed a Kisumu solution

NyumbaKisumu was started out of a genuine frustration with how difficult it was to find a decent, affordable apartment in Kisumu and its surrounding areas without going through a chain of agents — each taking a cut.

The idea was simple: build a clean, honest listing platform where property owners can post directly and buyers can find exactly what they need without the noise. Not a national platform stretched thin across the whole country, but something specifically built for the Kisumu area and the people who live here.

What we believe
Everyone deserves fair housing access
Finding a home should not depend on who you know or how much you can pay an agent upfront.
Sellers should keep what they earn
A flat listing fee means no surprise deductions when a deal closes. What you earn, you keep.
Local platforms serve local needs better
A platform built specifically for Kisumu understands the areas, the prices, and the people — national platforms don't.
Transparency builds trust
Prices, contacts, and terms shown clearly. No bait-and-switch, no hidden fees on either side.
What drives us

Our values

These are not aspirations — they are the actual decisions we make every day when building NyumbaKisumu.

Honesty first
We charge a clear flat fee. We show you who the seller is. We don't inflate prices or take hidden commissions. What you see is exactly what it is.
Community first
NyumbaKisumu was built specifically for Western Kenya. We understand Maseno student housing, Milimani luxury apartments, and everything in between.
Speed and simplicity
Post a listing in under 10 minutes. Contact a seller with one click. No forms with 20 fields, no account approval delays, no unnecessary steps.
M-Pesa native
We know how Kenya pays. Listing fees are paid via M-Pesa — straightforward, instant, and accessible to every seller with a phone.
Full visibility
Every listing shows real photos, real prices, and real contact details. Buyers know exactly what they're enquiring about before they make a single call.
Local knowledge
From Kondele to Riat Hills, we know which areas suit students, families, and professionals. That local context shapes every feature we build.

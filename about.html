Our mission

Connect people to homes across Kenya — without the middleman

Property hunting in Kenya has always worked the same way: you need a home, you call an agent, the agent calls another agent, and by the time you see the apartment you owe someone a commission before you've even moved in. It's a system that benefits everyone except the people actually looking for a home.

Nyumba254 cuts through all of that. Sellers post their properties directly for a flat fee. Buyers browse, shortlist, and contact sellers with one click — no intermediaries, no hidden costs, no wasted afternoons on viewings that were never a good match.

We believe the rental and property market in every corner of Kenya — not just the big cities — deserves a modern platform that puts landlords and tenants in the same room, digitally, and then gets out of the way.

How we got here

A problem we saw everywhere in Kenya, not just one city

Nyumba254 started out of a genuine frustration with how difficult it is to find a decent, affordable apartment anywhere in Kenya without going through a chain of agents — each taking a cut. This wasn't a one-city problem; it's how the market works almost everywhere.

The idea was simple: build a clean, honest listing platform where property owners can post directly and buyers can find exactly what they need without the noise — designed from day one to work for any town or county in Kenya, not tied to a single city.

What we believe

What drives us

Our values

These are not aspirations — they are the actual decisions we make every day when building Nyumba254.

Honesty first
We charge a clear flat fee. We show you who the seller is. We don't inflate prices or take hidden commissions. What you see is exactly what it is.
Community first
Nyumba254 is built for every community in Kenya. We understand student housing in Maseno just as well as luxury apartments in Nairobi's Kilimani.
Speed and simplicity
Post a listing in under 10 minutes. Contact a seller with one click. No forms with 20 fields, no account approval delays, no unnecessary steps.
M-Pesa native
We know how Kenya pays. Listing fees are paid via M-Pesa — straightforward, instant, and accessible to every seller with a phone, wherever they are.
Full visibility
Every listing shows real photos, real prices, and real contact details. Buyers know exactly what they're enquiring about before they make a single call.
Growing county by county
From Kondele to Kilimani to Nyali, we add new areas deliberately — so every county we cover gets the same quality of listings and local relevance.
